# Chunking & Formatting Strategy

## Chunking Approach
- Balanced chunk size to retain semantic meaning
- Overlapping chunks where required to preserve context continuity
- Metadata tagging for improved retrieval filtering

## Formatting Rules
- Consistent text structure
- Removal of headers, footers, and noise
- Logical grouping of related information

## Impact
Optimized chunking significantly improved:
- Retrieval relevance
- Context alignment
- Overall response quality
