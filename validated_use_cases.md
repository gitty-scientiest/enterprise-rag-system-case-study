# Validated Use Cases

## Use Case 1: Enterprise Knowledge Retrieval
Enable employees to ask natural language questions and receive
accurate answers grounded in internal documentation.

## Use Case 2: Department-Specific Assistance
Provide role-aware responses tailored to different business units
while respecting contextual relevance.

## Use Case 3: Decision Support
Support faster decision-making by surfacing relevant historical
information and structured insights.

---

## Validation Approach
- Stakeholder feedback
- Response accuracy checks
- Hallucination monitoring
- Iterative refinement

---

## Reliability Note
All use cases were validated through controlled testing and feedback
loops before being considered production-ready.
